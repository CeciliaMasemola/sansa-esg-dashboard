SANSA ESG Commitments & Public Policies

Our Commitments:

  • Net Zero by 2050: Committed to achieving carbon neutrality across all operations through renewable energy adoption and efficiency improvements
  • Circular Economy: Target 80% waste diversion from landfill by 2030 through enhanced recycling and composting programs
  • Water Stewardship: Reduce water consumption by 20% per employee by 2028 through conservation technologies and awareness programs
  • Social Impact: Maintain >80% employee satisfaction and achieve zero harm workplace through continuous safety improvements
  • Transparent Governance: Annual ESG reporting aligned with GRI Standards and regular stakeholder engagement
  • Biodiversity Protection: Complete invasive species removal and native habitat restoration across all sites by 2026
